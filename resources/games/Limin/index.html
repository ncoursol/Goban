Limin Cup

The Limin Cup (利民杯, in full 利民杯世界围棋星锐最强战 Limin Cup World Go Rising Stars Championship) is a Go tournament for young professional players (under 20, if not western) held in Hangzhou, China.

Chinese rules are followed. In particular, komi is 7.5. Time allotted is 1h in the preliminary rounds, 2h in the main tournament.

The main tournament has 24 (for the 1st edition) or 32 participants, playing in 5 rounds. There are two preliminary qualifying stages. For each qualifying stage the players are split up into six or eight groups of players. Each group plays a 5-round swiss tournament of which the top 4 finishers can continue to the next phase, which is a 2-round knockout tournament, where the six or eight winners participate in the main tournament. Players who did not win a place in the main tournament in Stage 1, can try again in Stage 2.
