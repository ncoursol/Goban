17x17 Tianyi Go by Golaxy

In Jan 2019, the Golaxy program played 17 Go games on a 17x17 board against 10 Chinese pros, 2 Japanese pros, 2 female pros and 3 amateur players. It won the challenge 16-1, only losing to Yang Dong 4p.

The games were played following Tianyi (天弈) rules: the first 4 stones are played at random places (but not on the first line), afterwards a komi auction determines who will play Black, and finally Golaxy pays 10 points additional komi to a pro, and 12 points to an amateur.

Here are the games, together with information about the komi auctions. The first mentioned bid is by Golaxy.
